## â“ FAQ

### Do I need a Phantom wallet?
**No!** You can use the platform without a wallet. Badges still appear on your profile.

### What if I connect my wallet later?
**All your existing badges will be minted as NFTs** when you first connect.

### Can I disconnect my wallet?
**Yes**, but your wallet address stays linked to your account (so you can reconnect and claim future badges).

### What if minting fails?
The badge is still earned and appears on your profile. NFT minting failure doesn't affect your progress.

### Can I transfer my NFTs?
**Yes!** Once in your wallet, you can:
- Transfer to another wallet
- Sell on NFT marketplaces
- Display in your Phantom wallet
- Use in other Solana dApps

### Do NFTs expire?
**No!** NFTs are permanent and stored on the blockchain forever.

### Can I claim NFTs for old badges?
**Yes!** When you connect your wallet, all unclaimed badges automatically become NFTs.

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### "Phantom wallet not found"
- Install the Phantom extension from [phantom.app](https://phantom.app)
- Refresh the page
- Make sure Phantom is enabled in your browser

### "Failed to connect wallet"
- Check that Phantom is unlocked
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
- Clear browser cache and try again

### "Wallet already connected to another account"
- Each wallet can only be linked to one account
- Use a different wallet or contact support

### NFT not showing in wallet
- Wait a few minutes (blockchain confirmation)
- Refresh your Phantom wallet
- Check the "Collectibles" tab in Phantom

### Badge earned but no NFT
- Make sure your wallet is connected
- Try manually claiming via the profile page
- Check if minting failed (badge still counts!)
